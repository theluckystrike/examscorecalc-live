Frequently asked

What is a good score on AP English Literature?

On AP English Literature, about 8% of students earn the top score of 5, and a 3 is the passing score, per recent College Board data. An estimated 73% composite reaches a 5 and 46% reaches a 3.

How is AP English Literature scored?

Your raw MC and free-response points combine into a weighted composite (MC ~45%, FRQ ~55%), then map to the 1-5 scale. The exact mapping changes slightly each year, so this is an estimate.

Is this the official Lit curve?

No. College Board does not publish exact annual cut scores. These are estimates based on publicly released scoring worksheets, updated for 2025-26.

When do AP scores come out?

AP exams are in May and scores typically release in early-to-mid July. Use this calculator beforehand to estimate where you'll land.
