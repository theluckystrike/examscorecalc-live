Frequently asked

What is a good SAT score?

On the SAT's 400 to 1600 scale, a 1200 or higher is competitive for most four-year colleges and 1400 or higher is strong. The recent national average is near 1050.

How is the digital SAT scored?

Each section (Reading & Writing, Math) is scored 200-800 and the two are added for a 400-1600 total. The digital SAT is section-adaptive, so conversion depends on the test form, and the number here is a representative estimate.

Is this the official SAT scale?

No. Because the digital SAT is adaptive and each form has its own curve, no single raw-to-scaled table is official. These are estimates.
