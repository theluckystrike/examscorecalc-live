Frequently asked

What is a good ACT score?

On the ACT's 1 to 36 composite scale, a 24 or higher is competitive for most colleges and 30 or higher is strong. The recent national average is near 20.

How is the ACT composite calculated?

Each of the four sections is converted from raw correct answers to a 1-36 scale, and your composite is the average of the four, rounded to the nearest whole number.

Is this the official ACT scale?

No. ACT raw-to-scale conversions vary by test form. These curves are representative estimates.
